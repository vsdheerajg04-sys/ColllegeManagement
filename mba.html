About MBA

MBA (Master of Business Administration) is a 2-year postgraduate program focused on business management, leadership, and entrepreneurship.

MBA Specializations

Marketing

Focuses on advertising, sales strategies, and market research.

Finance

Deals with banking, investment, accounting, and financial management.

Human Resources

Focuses on employee management, recruitment, and organizational behavior.

Operations

Focuses on supply chain management and business operations.
